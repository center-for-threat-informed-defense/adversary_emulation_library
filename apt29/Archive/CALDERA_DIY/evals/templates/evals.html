ATT&CK | Evals - APT 29

About

This CALDERA plugin is meant to emulate the techniques used by the MITRE ATT&CK team in ATT&CK evaluations.
Multiple CALDERA adversary profiles have been developed for both APT3 and APT29.
Consult the evals' plugin README.md for environment setup guidance. For general CALDERA questions, consult the CALDERA wiki .
